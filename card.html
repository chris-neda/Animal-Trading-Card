Macaque

Macaques are the most widespread primate genius, from Japan over the Indian Subcontinent to Northern Africa and Southern Europe.

  • Scientic Name: Macaca sylvanus
  • Max. Weight: 18kg
  • Max. Height: 0.76m
  • Average Lifespan: 20 yrs

The are 23 counted species of macaques, mostly spread through far east Asia and northern Africa. All macaques are organized in female dominated social groups.
